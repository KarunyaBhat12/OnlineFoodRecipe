Ingredients
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of water
How to Make
  1. Mix the flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water and stir until you get a smooth batter.
  3. Pour the batter into a pan and cook over medium heat.
  4. Cook until golden brown and serve hot.
